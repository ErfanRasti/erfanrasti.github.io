  It is completely reusable, and I use all of these configurations on a bunch of systems.
  It includes lots of packages and tools:
<ul>
  <li>Hyprland</li>
  <li>Neovim</li>
  <li>tmux</li>
  <li>waybar</li>
  <li>swaync</li>
  <li>swayosd</li>
  <li>matugen</li>
  <li>Zsh</li>
  <li>fish</li>
  <li>GTK</li>
</ul>
